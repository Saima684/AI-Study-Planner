🔒 Privacy Policy for AI Study Planner

Welcome to AI Study Planner 📚! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

📊 1. Information We Collect

AI Study Planner is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • 📝 Study Data: Subjects, schedules, notes, and study sessions you create are stored locally on your device only.
  • 🖼️ Profile Photo: If you choose to set a profile photo, it is stored locally on your device. We do not upload it to any server.
  • 🚫 No Personal Information: We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

⚙️ 2. How We Use Your Information

All data you enter in AI Study Planner is used solely to provide you with the app's features:

  • To organize your study schedule and subjects 📅
  • To track your study progress and history 📈
  • To send you study reminders and notifications (locally on your device) 🔔

💾 3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure Android Room Database. We do not have access to your data, and it is never transmitted to any external servers or cloud services. 🔐 Secure

🤝 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties. Your study data remains on your device at all times. 🛡️ Private

📢 5. Advertising

AI Study Planner uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers: AdMob may use advertising ID for ad personalization
  • General device info: Device type, OS version, screen size
  • Ad interaction data: Whether an ad was shown or clicked

We use only G-rated ads (suitable for all ages). We do NOT allow ads that are violent, sexual, or inappropriate for students.

Ad types used in this app:

  • Banner Ads: Small ads displayed at the bottom of the screen
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown between natural app transitions (with at least 60 seconds between each)
  • App Open Ads: Ads shown when the app opens or returns to the foreground

You can opt out of personalized ads by resetting your advertising ID in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID.

For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please visit: Google's Advertising Policies

🔑 6. Permissions We Request

  • 🔔 Notifications: To send you study reminders and schedule alerts
  • 📸 Camera: To capture your profile photo (optional)
  • 📳 Vibration: For notification haptic feedback
  • ⏰ Alarms: To schedule exact study reminders
  • 🌐 Internet & Network State: Required for loading advertisements

👶 7. Children's Privacy

AI Study Planner is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal data at all, our app is safe for students of any age. All ads shown are G-rated (suitable for general audiences). 🧒 Safe for Kids

🗑️ 8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app data from your device settings ⚙️
  • Uninstalling the app (this removes all local data) 🗑️

🛡️ 9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using a screen lock on your device. 🔒 Local Storage

🔄 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
